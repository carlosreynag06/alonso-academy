## `luma.interactions.storyboard.v1`

Using the cast anchor, create four equal landscape vignettes in a 2-by-2 sheet:

1. Listen and find: Miko listens beside three large real-object picture tiles—red apple, blue ball, green book—and points toward the apple while all choices stay visible.
2. Character conversation: Miko and Nia face each other at Welcome Dock; Miko waves and Nia visibly listens before taking a turn.
3. Story and retell: Nia presents three sequential picture cards at Story Camp—boat leaves, finds a red ball, returns—while Miko listens and points through the sequence.
4. Learned-word gallery and completion: Moss places an apple picture into the gallery while Pippa and Miko share a gentle high-five and Nia responds warmly.

Make every learning action understandable through image, gesture, and eye line alone. No text, speech bubbles, recording device, score, prize, coin, star reward, or extra characters.

**Post-generation instructional restriction:** the first vignette reveals the apple through Miko’s point. It is therefore a non-scored demonstration frame only. It must never be used for an independent or scored listen-and-find choice, and parent approval of the sheet cannot broaden that permitted use. A later scored frame must use a new neutrally posed asset version with no answer cue.
